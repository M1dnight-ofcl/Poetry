Who Is Edgar Allan Poe?

Edgar Allan Poe is a poet and writer from the 1800s. He is well known for his poetry and short stories. Most of his stories are about crime and mystery. His most famous poems are "The Raven," "Eldorado," and "A Dream Within a Dream."
