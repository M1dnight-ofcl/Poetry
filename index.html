Famous Poets

There are many well known poets that have come and gone. some of the most recognizable include William Shakespeare, Edgar Allan Poe, and Maya Angelou. To learn more about poets like these, scroll below.

William Shakespeare

William Shakespeare is a very well-known poet from the 1500s to the 1600s. He is renowned for writing plays and poems. He composed many sonnets during his lifetime, most of which had generic titles such as "Sonnet 73" or "Sonnet 82". He has more than a hundred sonnets with names like this. He is regarded as one of the greatest writers to have ever lived.

Edgar Allan Poe

Edgar Allan Poe is a poet and writer from the 1800s. He is well known for his poetry and short stories. Most of his stories are about crime and mystery. His most famous poems are "The Raven," "Eldorado," and "A Dream Within a Dream."
